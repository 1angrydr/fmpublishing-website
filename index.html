Frequently Asked Questions

Is EZPZ Trade Journal a trading platform?

No. EZPZ is a calculator and journal, not a trading platform. You cannot buy, sell, or execute trades within the app. It's designed to help you track trades you make on external platforms (like MT4, Binance, etc.) and calculate proper position sizes.

Does the app connect to any exchanges or brokers?

No. The app works 100% offline. There are no API connections, no cloud storage, and no external integrations. You manually enter all data.

How does pricing work?

One-time purchase. No subscriptions, no recurring fees. Pay once, own forever.

What data does the app collect?

None. All your trade data is stored locally on your device using iOS AppStorage. FM Publishing does not have access to your trading information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use this for stocks or options?

Currently, EZPZ Trade Journal is optimized for forex and cryptocurrency trading. Stock and options support may be added in future updates based on user feedback.
